Akan Names are used by the Ghanians to name their children depending on the day they were born.According to Akan tradition, each child is born with a name associated to their soul.These namimg system was founded by the ancestors and has since gained popularity among many and they desire to learn more about the origin of the Akan Names. These names enable us to tell what led to person's birth, the day they were born and thier rank among their siblings. Ghanians view naming of their children as a very important rite of passage.
